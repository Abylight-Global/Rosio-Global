Our Services

Giving you financial power.

Category 01

Wealth Creation

Insurance, investment, and education products designed around your family's long-term security.

Life Insurance with Living Benefit

Provides financial protection to your loved ones in the event of your death, while also offering benefits — critical illness, long-term care, and disability income protection — you can use during your lifetime.

Term Insurance

An affordable way to protect your family, with flexible terms and coverage options so you can choose the policy that best suits your needs.

Travel / Health Insurance

Comprehensive policies covering medical expenses, trip cancellation, and lost luggage — essential for anyone planning a trip abroad.

Estate Planning

A personalized plan that ensures your assets are distributed according to your wishes and your loved ones are cared for.

Investments

RRSPs, TFSAs, and Guaranteed Income Supplements matched to your financial goals and risk tolerance — retirement, education, or a rainy day.

Children's Wealth Account

Set aside money for your child's future — education or other expenses — with the potential for tax-free growth.

Children's Education Account / RESP

Helps you save for your child's education tax-free, with access to government grants and incentives.

Category 02

Academy

Education, mentorship, and resources built to strengthen financial literacy and understanding.

Workshop and Seminar

Live sessions tailored to individuals, families, and businesses, helping participants build practical financial knowledge and reach their educational goals with the right support and guidance.

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance from experienced advisors, helping you apply our philosophies — Multihanded Income, Self Improvement, and 10%, 3 Rules, 3 Goals — to your own financial journey.

Other Library

A growing collection of guides and resources — including Navigating Your Financial Future — designed to help you build financial confidence at every stage of your journey.
