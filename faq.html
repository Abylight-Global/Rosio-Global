Frequently Asked Questions

Answers, in plain language.

ROSIO Concepts Inc offers a range of financial services including life insurance, term insurance, travel/health insurance, estate planning, investments, and more. We're dedicated to helping individuals in Canada achieve their financial goals through tailored solutions and personalized support.

We offer life insurance with living benefits, term insurance, and travel/health insurance — each designed to protect your family's financial security while fitting your budget and circumstances.

Our team aims to respond to all inquiries within one to two business days. For urgent matters, we recommend reaching out by phone or booking a session directly.

We work with you to build a retirement strategy using RRSPs, TFSAs, and other investment vehicles suited to your timeline, goals, and risk tolerance — with ongoing guidance as your plan evolves.

A Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) lets your investments grow and be withdrawn tax-free, making it a flexible tool for both short- and long-term savings goals.

Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) and Good Production Practices (GPP) are standards that help ensure your products consistently meet quality and safety requirements. Our team helps you implement these protocols in your operations.

Yes — we work with entrepreneurs and business owners on financial planning, insurance, and regulatory or quality assurance needs as they build and grow their businesses.

Estate planning ensures your assets are distributed according to your wishes and that your loved ones are provided for. Our team builds a personalized plan around your unique needs and goals.

Our Children's Education Account / RESP options let you save for your child's education with the potential for tax-free growth and access to government grants and incentives.

Quality Assurance and Quality Control protocols help ensure your products and services consistently meet industry standards and regulations, protecting both your customers and your reputation.
